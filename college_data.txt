The Neotia University is a private university located in Sarisha, near Diamond Harbour Road, South 24 Parganas, West Bengal, India.

The Neotia University was established in 2014 under the West Bengal State Government Act and started its academic session in 2015.

The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act.

The campus of The Neotia University is spread over approximately 60 acres of land with modern infrastructure and facilities.

The university provides courses in engineering, management, science, law, pharmacy, agriculture, maritime studies, and humanities.

Popular courses include B.Tech, BBA, MBA, B.Pharm, B.Sc, BBA LLB, BA LLB, and Nautical Science.

The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD programs.

The admission process is based on merit and entrance exams depending on the course.

The university provides hostel facilities for both boys and girls with residential blocks on campus.

The campus includes academic buildings, sports facilities, libraries, laboratories, and cultural activity areas.

The university has a dedicated placement cell that helps students with training, internships, and job opportunities.

The Neotia University recorded around 71 to 80 percent placement rate in recent years.

In 2024, around 503 students registered for placements and about 362 students were placed in companies.

The average salary package for students is around 3 to 5 lakh per annum.

The average package for CSE Data Science students is around 4.2 lakh per annum.

The highest salary package offered can go up to around 9 to 10 lakh per annum depending on the course.

Top recruiters include TCS, Wipro, Infosys, HCL, Tech Mahindra, Amazon, Deloitte, and Cognizant.

The university provides internship opportunities across multiple departments.

Engineering courses have high placement rates with average packages above 4 lakh per annum.

Hospitality and management courses have near 100 percent placement rates in some years.

The university also offers maritime studies with international placement opportunities in shipping companies.

The Neotia University has collaborations with industries for skill-based education and training.

The university organizes cultural events, technical fests, and extracurricular activities regularly.

Facilities include cafeteria, medical support, sports complexes, auditorium, and research labs.

The university has departments such as Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Biotechnology, Agriculture, and Law.

The campus environment is green and well-maintained with modern amenities.

Students can apply for admission online through the official website of the university.

The university aims to provide industry-oriented education and practical learning experience.
