The Neotia University, also called TNU, is a private university located at Sarisa, Diamond Harbour Road, South 24 Parganas, West Bengal 743368.

The Neotia University was established in 2014 under the West Bengal State Government Act and started its academic session in 2015.

The Neotia University is recognized by the University Grants Commission under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act.

The Neotia University is part of the Ambuja Neotia Group and offers industry-oriented undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral programs.

The campus is spread across about 60 acres and includes academic buildings, laboratories, a library, sports facilities, hostels, cafeteria, healthcare support, and activity spaces.

For 2026-27 admissions, The Neotia University has announced admissions for several regular and lateral entry programs, and admission enquiry numbers include 7044446888 and 7044446999.

Students can apply online through the TNU admissions portal or the official website, and admission counsellors can help applicants complete the form.

The admission process generally includes filling the application form, paying the application fee, appearing for the online admission entrance test where required, receiving the result, provisional admission, document verification, and final admission.

The TNU admission process for 2026-27 mentions a non-refundable application fee of Rs. 500.

For provisional admission, students may pay a provisional admission fee of Rs. 10,000, subject to course availability and eligibility.

At final admission, the university verifies eligibility documents and the remaining first-semester fee must be paid before a UID number is generated.

The admission test result is generally available on the student dashboard within 24 hours, and 40 percent is mentioned as the qualifying mark for the admission test.

For many undergraduate courses, the entrance examination period for 2026 admissions is listed from March 2026 to July 2026, and the final admission deadline follows UGC guidelines.

The Neotia University offers courses through schools such as Science and Technology, Integrated Sciences, Health Sciences, Hospitality and Culinary Art, Humanities Management and Social Sciences, Legal Studies, Maritime Studies, Nursing, Pharmacy, Agriculture and Allied Sciences, Fisheries Science, and Skill Development and Vocational Studies.

School of Science and Technology courses include B.Tech in CSE with AI and Robotics, B.Tech in CSE with AI and Cyber Security, B.Tech in CSE with AI and Data Science, B.Tech in CSE with AI and Machine Learning, Bachelor of Computer Application, MCA, M.Tech in AI and ML, Diploma in AI and ML, and lateral entry B.Tech options.

B.Tech CSE with AI and ML requires 10+2 Science with Physics and Mathematics mandatory plus Chemistry, Computer Science, Computer Applications, Biotechnology, Biology, or Technical Vocational, or a 3-year engineering diploma; the official eligibility page lists 60 percent marks.

B.Tech CSE with AI and ML has a semester fee of Rs. 65,000, one-time fee of Rs. 29,000, first semester fee of Rs. 94,000, 8 semesters, and total course fee of Rs. 5,49,000.

B.Tech CSE with AI and ML requires the university's own entrance exam, and no other fees are mentioned on the official course FAQ besides the listed admission and semester fees.

B.Tech CSE specializations at TNU include Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, Artificial Intelligence and Cyber Security, and Artificial Intelligence and Robotics.

Bachelor of Computer Application, or BCA, is a 3+1 year undergraduate program focused on computer applications and software development.

BCA eligibility at TNU is 10+2 in Science, Arts, Commerce, or related stream with 55 percent marks according to the official eligibility list.

Diploma in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning requires Class 10 passed with Mathematics and Science subjects and at least 50 percent marks.

Diploma in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning has a semester fee of Rs. 25,000, one-time fee of Rs. 29,000, first semester fee of Rs. 54,000, and total course fee of Rs. 1,79,000.

School of Integrated Sciences courses include B.Sc. Hons or Hons with Research in Biotechnology, B.Sc. Hons or Hons with Research in Microbiology, M.Sc. Biotechnology, and M.Sc. Microbiology.

B.Sc. Biotechnology and B.Sc. Microbiology eligibility is 10+2 Science with 55 percent marks and any three science subjects.

M.Sc. Biotechnology eligibility is B.Sc. with 55 percent marks in life science subjects such as Botany, Zoology, Microbiology, Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Agricultural Sciences, or related subjects, plus personal interview.

M.Sc. Biotechnology has a semester fee of Rs. 40,000, one-time fee of Rs. 29,000, first semester fee of Rs. 69,000, 4 semesters, and total course fee of Rs. 1,89,000.

School of Maritime Studies courses include B.Tech Marine Engineering, B.Sc. Nautical Science, Post Graduate Diploma in Marine Engineering, Diploma in Nautical Science, Orientation Course for Catering Personnel, and lateral entry Marine Engineering.

B.Sc. Nautical Science eligibility includes 10+2 Science with 60 percent in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, 50 percent in English at Class 10 or Class 12, valid IMU-CET rank, passport, age 18 to 25 years, and medical fitness as per D.G. Shipping requirements.

B.Sc. Nautical Science has a semester fee of Rs. 1,61,500, one-time fee of Rs. 92,000, first semester fee of Rs. 2,53,500, and total course fee of Rs. 10,61,000.

B.Sc. Nautical Science trains students in navigation, cargo work, marine command, seamanship, ship stability, collision prevention, maritime law, meteorology, and ship operation technology.

TNU maritime industry partners include K-Ship, Destan Ship Management Pvt. Ltd., and Blue Bird Shipping Management Private Limited for marine and nautical placement support.

School of Pharmacy courses include Bachelor of Pharmacy, Diploma of Pharmacy, Bachelor of Pharmacy lateral entry, M.Pharm specializations, and Ph.D. in Pharmacy.

Bachelor of Pharmacy at TNU is approved by the Pharmacy Council of India and recognized by the Government of West Bengal.

Bachelor of Pharmacy has a semester fee of Rs. 85,000, one-time fee of Rs. 29,000, first semester fee of Rs. 1,14,000, and total course fee of Rs. 7,09,000; OJT or related exam fee is included.

School of Nursing courses include B.Sc. Nursing and General Nursing Midwifery, also called GNM.

General Nursing Midwifery has a semester fee of Rs. 60,000, one-time fee of Rs. 29,000, first semester fee of Rs. 89,000, and total course fee of Rs. 3,89,000.

School of Legal Studies courses include BBA LLB Hons, BA LLB Hons, LLM, and Ph.D. programmes in legal studies.

BA LLB Hons eligibility is 10+2 in any stream, including Science, Arts, or Commerce, with 55 percent marks.

BA LLB Hons has no entrance test according to the official course FAQ, and the semester fee is Rs. 40,000.

BA LLB Hons has Bar Council of India approval requirements, and TNU states it has approval for the course.

School of Humanities, Management and Social Sciences courses include BBA Hons, BBA Hons in Digital Marketing, BBA Hons in Logistics and Supply Chain Management, BBA Hons in Marketing Management, BBA Hons in Fintech, B.Com Hons in Banking and Capital Market, design programs, media studies, BA programs, MBA in Fintech, MBA in Marketing, MBA in HR, and Ph.D. options.

BBA Hons eligibility is 10+2 in Science, Arts, or Commerce with minimum 55 percent marks.

BBA Hons requires an online entrance test according to the official course FAQ.

BBA Hons has a semester fee of Rs. 49,500 and a one-time admission charge of Rs. 29,000 payable with the first semester fee.

BBA Hons one-time payment components include Rs. 10,000 admission processing fee, Rs. 10,000 refundable security deposit, Rs. 5,000 university registration fee, Rs. 3,000 development fee, and Rs. 1,000 alumni association subscription.

School of Health Sciences courses include Bachelor of Psychology, BMLT, BPT, Bachelor of Optometry, BMRIT, Operation Theatre and Anesthesia Technology, Critical Care Technology, Hospital Management, Nutrition and Dietetics, Rehabilitation Education, and related lateral entry and postgraduate programs.

Bachelor of Optometry eligibility is 12th standard with Physics, Chemistry, Biology or Mathematics and at least 50 percent marks.

Bachelor of Optometry requires an online entrance test and has a semester fee of Rs. 55,000 plus a one-time admission fee of Rs. 29,000.

Bachelor of Anesthesia and Operation Theatre Technology requires 10+2 with minimum 55 percent marks in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English.

Bachelor of Anesthesia and Operation Theatre Technology requires an online entrance test and has a semester fee of Rs. 45,000 plus a one-time admission fee of Rs. 29,000.

School of Hospitality and Culinary Art courses include B.Sc. Hospitality and Hotel Administration, B.Sc. Hotel Administration and Culinary Art, Bachelor of Travel and Tourism Administration, MBA Hospitality Management, and Ph.D. options.

School of Agriculture and Allied Sciences courses include B.Sc. Hons Agriculture, B.Voc Agriculture, B.Sc. Hons Agribusiness Management, Diploma in Agriculture, MBA Agri Business and Rural Management, and M.Sc. Agriculture specializations.

School of Fisheries Science courses include Bachelor of Fisheries Science, Master of Fisheries Science, and Ph.D. in Aquaculture.

Bachelor of Fisheries Science eligibility is 12th standard with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology or Mathematics with 55 percent marks, and an online admission entrance test is required.

Bachelor of Fisheries Science has a semester fee of Rs. 31,500 or Rs. 35,000 depending on the official course page version, plus a one-time fee of Rs. 29,000; students should confirm the latest amount with TNU admissions.

TNU scholarship options for 2026-27 include Chancellor's Scholarship, Gramotthan Scholarship, Special Hill Students' Scholarship, Sahodarya Scholarship, Mere Apne Scholarship, Sports Scholarship, and Cultural Scholarship.

The Chancellor's Scholarship can provide 50 percent to 100 percent tuition fee scholarship, subject to conditions such as family income less than Rs. 2.5 lakh, interview, and document verification.

Several TNU scholarships offer 25 percent tuition fee scholarship for eligible students, such as Gramotthan, Special Hill Students, Sahodarya, Mere Apne, Sports, and Cultural scholarships.

TNU states scholarships are generally calculated on tuition fee, not necessarily on the full total course fee.

TNU fees can be paid by demand draft, cheque, or online payment through the TNU website or payment gateway.

TNU fee payment details shown on official course pages include payment in the name of The Neotia University.

The official course pages list Bank of Baroda, account number 6595020000033, IFSC BARB0VJOKOL, SME Branch Kolkata 700001 for payments; students should verify bank details before paying.
